Telechips aux_detect device

Required property:
- compatible:
	telechips,aux_detect

- pintctrl-0:
	<&aux_detect_pin>

- aux-gpios:
	aux detect gpio

Example:
	aux_detect {
		compatible = "telechips,aux_detect";
		status = "okay";

		pinctrl-names = "default";
		pinctrl-0 = <&aux_detect_pin>;

		aux-gpios = <&gpmb 11 1>;
		aux-active = <1>;
	};
